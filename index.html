Hope in Darkness

In the world of novels and movies, we often come across various characters and while some aren't that reletable, some actually leave an impressionable imapct on us and manage to stay with us long after we are done watching a series or movie or reading a book.

Though most narratives never cease to highlight the contrasting difference between light and dark, some actually realise that world isn't as light or dark, there are shades of grey. These shows delve deeper into charachter development and give its audiences a true understanding of each charachter's circumstances. In these portayals all the questions of viewers or readers are answered and that makes these shows so special in people's heart.

The shades of grey sets a tone in the narrative of a plot and is often traced back to the popular chinese philosophy of yin and yang. The realism that is adapted, makes the leads not so ideally perfect and much more human. This website is solely dedicated to highlight the most underrated characters and to celebrate the light in their darkness.

The characters we will focus on are :

Draco Lucius Malfoy
The famous antagonist of Harry Potter series. While his bullying others as a child is quite distasteful, he later grew up to be a man who was a victim of his circumstances.
Kylo Ren
The grandson of Darth Vader himself, Kylo Ren is a complex charachter who was subjected to mistrust and wariness as a child. His master and his uncle Luke Skywalker attempted to kill him and thus he succumbed to the seduction Snoke.
Lucifer Morningstar
The devil himself has shown over a period of time some salvageable qualities
